Question 3: What are the signs of general ill health in a small animal (Physical and behavioural)?

Click bellow to see the answer

Question 4: Explain the main difference between monogastric and ruminant digestive systems.

Click bellow to see the answer

Monogastric animals have a single-chambered stomach (e.g., dogs, cats), while ruminants have a four-compartment digestive system (e.g., cattle).
